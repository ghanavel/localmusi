We collect information at various points including when you download, install and run the Musiguru Applications or register or contact us via our Website. While we limit the collection of personally identifiable information we may collect information such as:

  • Your device information.
  • Content generated by you that you choose to share or upload to our servers.

  • Analytical logging that provide us information on the various steps you take when you use our applications for the sole purpose of understanding and improving the applications.

What do we use that information for?
We use this information primarily for the purpose of improving your experience from the use of our application such as:
Use your contact or email address to respond to your queries or provide you any notifications or updates.
Use your device, crash reports and analytical logging data to address issues or to provide new and innovative features derived from your usage of the application.
We may secondarily use data at an aggregate, non personally identifiable level for various analytics and/or creation of relevant industry reports when needed.
We do not sell your personally identifiable information to any third party.

How do we protect your information?
We use a variety of security measures to protect your information both on the device as well as on our servers.
