Sydney :  
Musiguru presents the next edition of our unique and well received Carnatic Symphony

The Carnatic Symphony, a unique idea conceptualized by Sri K.N.Shashikiran, fuses Western Classical with Carnatic music. It will have kids from various nationalities and musical backgrounds performing together. This concert will feature simple yet attractive pieces from Carnatic music such as short Utsava Sampradaya kritis of Sri Tyagaraja, Nottu svarams of Sri Muttusvamy Dikshitar and other similarly popular pieces presented through voice and instruments.

This Symphony has already been featured in the prestigious Cleveland Thyagaraja Festival, 2013. Participants will be awarded special medals and certificates at the end of the performance and will get global media visibility via television and YouTube channels.

We invite all music students, including those at beginner levels, to participate in this great opportunity.

Cleveland :  
Musiguru presents the world’s first ever Carnatic Symphony at the Cleveland Thyagaraja Festival 2013.

The Carnatic Symphony, a unique idea conceptualized by Sri K.N.Shashikiran, fuses Western Classical with Carnatic music. It will have kids from various nationalities and musical backgrounds performing together. This concert will feature simple yet attractive pieces from Carnatic music such as short Utsava Sampradaya kritis of Sri Tyagaraja, Nottu svarams of Sri Muttusvamy Dikshitar and other similarly popular pieces presented through voice and instruments.

The Carnatic Symphony, an Indo-American collaboration, in honor of World leader Barack Obama, is a cultural gesture to promote world peace and harmony.

This Symphony will be featured in the prestigious Cleveland Thyagaraja Festival, 2013 and will be publicized at the Senate level and will draw national attention. Participants will be awarded special medals and certificates at the end of the performance and will get global media visibility via television and YouTube channels.

The Carnatic Symphony will also be conducted in various other locations in North America.

We invite all music students, including those at beginner levels, to participate in this great opportunity.
