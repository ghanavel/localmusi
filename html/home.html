Comprehensive Carnatic Curriculum
Musiguru has a constantly growing library of content focused on creating a comprehensive curriculum for learning Carnatic music. Starting from the Basics with interesting "Ear training exercises" for beginners to "Ragam Tanam Pallavis" for intermediates to voice culture exercises for advanced learners.

The Carnatic syllabus is designed and curated by Shri K. N. Shashikiran

FAQ
  1. Where do I download the app from?
    Our app is currently available in the Apple Store here.
  2. What musical forms or instruments are available?
    The Musiguru app series will introduce learning for a number of musical forms or instruments on an ongoing basis. You can preview the currently available courses in the Lesson Library above.
  3. I have feedback on the application, who can I contact?
    We value feedback from our users and believe that it will be a critical part of how the app shapes up going forward. You can email us at musiguru@levitum.com with your feedback.
  4. I am a musician and am interested in providing lessons through Musiguru?
    We will be more than happy to discuss how your training lessons can be provided through Musiguru. Please contact us at "musiguru@levitum.com" with subject "Musician Inquiry" or click here.
